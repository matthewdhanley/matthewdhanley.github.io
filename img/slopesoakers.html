RED BULL SLOPESOAKERS RETURNS TO COPPER MOUNTAIN

Come close out snow season with a splash and take a chance at Red Bull Slopesoakers! Open to both ski and snowboard categories, this unique contest combines a Slopestyle Rail Jam and a Pond Skim. Anyone (16+) who wants to test the waters can register abovefor$25.


2017 will have larger ponds and bigger features so the chances to get wet are greater than ever before. Come one, come all, bring a guest, and hang loose. This is going to be epic!

FAQ'S

How does it work?

Jam Format 4 HEATS (20 mintues each) with a 20 Minute Final

How are you judged?

Participants will be judged on the following criteria, earning up to ten points for each category.


1. Tricks & Style

2. Skimming Steez

3. Crowd Reaction

4. Risk/Reward - Skimming and not just gapping the ponds


What are the rules?

Helmets are required - No exceptions!

No ski poles allowed
